Project information

  • Category:2D,Space Shooter
  • Client: ASU Company
  • Project date: 01 March, 2020
  • Source Code: Github
  • Play here: itch.io

Main Objective

You are a noob trying to win against your enemies(Purus) by stealing as many coins as possible and dodging or killing enemies and finally escaping the purus maze.

What I did

  • Designed and developed the core functionalities such as movement, jumping,colliding,shooting and score system.
  • Designed and developed additional features such as coin pickups ,obstacles and bouncing materials.
  • Contructed an overall tilemap system with diferent layers for every type of tiles.
  • Implemented the player lives system with scene persists in the game levels.
  • Implemented a singleton pattern for the soundtrack player,Scene persists,level manager and score system.
  • Designed and developed the enemy behaviour in the levels.
  • Developed an effecient level manager for menus ,game levels and saving the score system.
  • Contructed a stable camera system for different states(idle,running and climbing) using the state cams of Cinemachine in Unity.
  • Contructed the overall level design of the game.
  • Worked on playtesting and overall balancing of the game.
